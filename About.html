I'm a passionate designer and developer who believes in creating digital experiences that are both beautiful and functional. My journey began with a curiosity about how things work, which evolved into a love for crafting user-centered solutions.

With over 5 years of experience in the digital space, I've had the opportunity to work with startups, agencies, and established companies, helping them translate their vision into engaging digital products.
